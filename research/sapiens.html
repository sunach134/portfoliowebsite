The Next Homo Sapiens
-An unpredictable query

Ever taken into account what the next generation Homo Sapiens would be like ? Is the neo technological advancement going to uplift the human civilization or ditch us even below where we are now? How idiotic would that be to not take into consideration the long run effects of vulnerable photon emission upon the human genes? What could be the daily activity of the human of the future? What are they likely to get engaged with as a part of running their economy?

In the next part, I will be presenting my perception on above unpredictable queries that we all present human possess inside our mind, but a very few of us attempt to scrutinize it.
