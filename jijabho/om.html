ॐ:

A Mantra That Terraformed My Life

" ॐ भूर्भुवः स्वः । तत्सवितुर्वरेण्यं भर्गो देवस्यः धीमहि । धियो यो नः प्रचोदयात् ।। "

Back in 2020, when the COVID-19 outbreak had kissed it's summit, the silence in the society too had kissed it's peak and people's movement had come to extreme halt.

In the midst of such an unfavourable situation for we humankind, I got the opportunity to explore the inner energy within me and got to feel the real power of the guru mantra; 'The Gayatri Mantra'.

Those days, when the cloudburst happened, I simply used to go to my open terrace and leave my body completely open under nature's lap, no matter how heavily it rained, how loud the clouds quarreled and how intense the sparkles produced were. Those days, I personally feel are the golden days of my life which terraformed my life to a different and even higher energy level. Even these days, whenever I chant the guru mantra or hear the chant from any other source, it just makes me feel so nostalgic and energises me with a different energy level. God !! What a 'powèrful mantrà' you've gifted to this mankind.

2079-06-08
Gairigaun, Kathmandu
